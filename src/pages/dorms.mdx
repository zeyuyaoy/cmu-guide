## Preface
The following data has been produced and maintained by current students. This rendition is based off of this
[spreadsheet](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1FkCaGv6nZAVP808w4iK7Awe5jEgMs-mg4xdmH-6jsr8/edit?usp=sharing).

All dorms come with laundry facilities. Prices are per year. Measurements in feet. For more official information visit
[the housing website](https://www.cmu.edu/housing/our-communities/first-year/index.html).

Capacity means total # of beds-- some may be taken up by upperclassmen if the note says "upperclassmen live here too". Bed counts for each are also available to view.
Default is that it's freshmen only.
[HOUSING PRICES AND CAPACITIES, 2026/2027](https://www.cmu.edu/housing/forms/26-27-housing-rates-non-staff.pdf)

Colors are used for Carnegie Cup during O-Week; not too important.
Confused by Traditional vs Semi-suite etc? Visit [Dorms Overview](/dorms-overview).
All communal bathrooms are staff-cleaned.
