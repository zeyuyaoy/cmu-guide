FOR REFERENCE: My freshman year I lived in mudge C1 in a semisuite double. I had a double room and shared a semiprivate bathroom with 3 other people. I am from California so I either had to pack stuff in my suitcases or buy it in Pittsburgh.

REMINDER: Think about storage when buying/bringing stuff! if you live farther than driving distance away and don't have a place to keep stuff over summer, it can be hard to move your things around so try not to accumulate too much stuff if that's a concern for you!

**Key**

- ✓✓✓ = necessary
- ✓✓ = very nice to have
- ✓ = helpful to have

---

### Bedding

- ✓✓✓ sheet set(I only had one and I was fine but get two if you want)
- ✓✓✓ comforter/duvet (get a duvet cover for extra protection if you have a nice one)
- ✓✓✓ mattress protector (I had one idk how useful it was but it might make you feel better to have an extra layer of protection if you don't trust the mattress)
- ✓✓✓ pillow(however many you want - I only had one! and throw pillows if you like the aesthetics but I didn't have enough storage for those)
- ✓✓✓ stuffed animal(absolutely necessary)(you will be sad if you don't bring one)
- ✓✓ mattress topper/pad (if you want - personally I didn't need one)
- ✓ throw blanket(great for cuddling up at your desk or in common areas)

### Appliances

- ✓✓✓ fan(if you have a no-ac dorm or for white noise if you're into that)(I used mine a lot) (i recommend the woozoo from costco due to it being small and compact and easy to store)
- ✓✓✓ chargers
- ✓✓✓ headphones
- ✓✓ extension cords(2) (so nice to have, I like to have one by my desk and one by my bed)(outlets are in wierd places sometimes)
- ✓✓ lamp(not necessary but nice to have)(I used mine a lot at night when my roommate was asleep)
- ✓ portable charger (if your phone dies quickly like mine)
- ✓ airpods/equivalent (you can get cheap airpods knockoffs on Amazon, or if you prefer wired ones those are good to have on hand)
- ✓ batteries(if you have appliances that use them)
- ✓ speaker(if you want)(really nice for vibes or to play outside on a nice day)
- ✓ a monitor if you use one/have one (for the CS majors/gamers)
- ✓ mini fridge(if you want to buy one)
- ✓ microwave(if you want to buy one)
- ✓ water kettle or coffee maker(if you need caffeine)(if shipping to school address make sure the wattage is allowed or they will flag it but what they don't know won't hurt them if you buy it elsewhere)
